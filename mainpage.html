Matching harvesters and growers

Arecanut harvesting and processing requires huge labour force and expensive machineries. Acknowledging these difficulties encountered by small scale Arecanut growers, we have designed a match making feature where Arecanut growers are matched to Arecanut harvesters who pay cash in exchange for the raw Arecanut produce harvested from growers field.


User of this feature has to choose whether they are Arecanut grower or harvester. Growers have to enter details of their field and its yield while harvesters have to enter their prefered location of field and target quantity of produce.The algorithm matches growers and harvesters based on the input details and shows the matched results with their contact information.
